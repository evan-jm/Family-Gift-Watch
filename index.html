Welcome to Family Gift Watch!

Once logged in, you will be able to see what gifts each individual has put on their Christmas list this year.

In addition, you will be able to see who has purchased any gifts for anyone in the system. This will prevent duplicate purchases occurring.

Also, users will never be allowed to view gifts that are purchased for them, to avoid spoiling any surprises.
